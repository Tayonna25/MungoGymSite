About Apex Performance

Our Mission

At Apex Performance Training, our mission is to transform lives through personalized coaching and next-level fitness experiences. We focus on empowering individuals of all fitness levels to reach their peak potential through dedication, education, and discipline.

Meet Coach Ray Mungo

Ray Mungo, the founder and lead coach of Apex Performance, brings a wealth of athletic and professional experience to the team. A graduate of Eastern Michigan University with a degree in Sports Management, Ray has played nearly every major sport competitively and understands what it takes to train like an athlete—both physically and mentally. With a coaching style that blends intensity, discipline, and encouragement, Ray believes in pushing clients just outside of their comfort zones to unlock sustainable growth and peak performance.

Our Philosophy

We don’t believe in cookie-cutter workouts. Every program is customized, every client is valued, and every goal is achievable with the right mindset and support. Whether you're an athlete looking to improve performance or someone starting your fitness journey, Apex is the place to build confidence and crush goals.

Why Choose Us?

With a background in sports, experience in performance training, and a commitment to holistic health, Apex Performance offers more than just a workout—we offer a lifestyle. Our community-centered approach, elite coaching, and results-driven programs set us apart.
